Things to do in Indianapolis

Make the most of your visit to the Crossroads of America!

  • Indianapolis Motor Speedway: Home of the Indy 500 and NASCAR races
  • White River State Park: Beautiful waterfront park with trails and attractions
  • Massachusetts Avenue: Trendy district with dining, shopping, and nightlife
  • Indianapolis Museum of Art: World-class art collection and stunning gardens
  • Monument Circle: Iconic downtown landmark and shopping district
  • Canal Walk: Scenic 3-mile loop perfect for walking or paddle boating
