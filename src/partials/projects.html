Family History Visualization

January 2021

Tool: d3.js

Inspired by Yannick Brouwer's family history project, I wanted to make my own. I wanted it to be able to be rendered nativaly in a browser which is what I used d3.js. d3.js is a powerful data visualization tool for javascript. All the data was manually pulled from Ancestry.com and ordered chronlogically to show the migration of my family over time.

I find it fasinating that so many people's story lead to where I am today and I wanted to share it with my family and others.
